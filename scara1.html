SCARA 1 — IISc thesis

Image-based visual servoing (IBVS) on a Mitsubishi SCARA at IISc Bangalore, with a custom-designed part holder. The robot is a commercial platform; the servoing control and the holder are my contribution.

LabIISc Bangalore · DACAS
PlatformMitsubishi SCARA (commercial)
My workIBVS control · holder design

Image-based visual servoing closes the control loop directly on features in the camera image rather than on estimated pose. On the Mitsubishi SCARA I implemented the servoing scheme as my thesis work, and designed a holder to present parts to the robot repeatably.
