Auxetic structures

End-to-end design, fabrication, and characterization of re-entrant lattices that grow wider when stretched — a negative Poisson's ratio — with a journal manuscript in preparation.

RoleDesign · fabrication · FEA · testing
Designs5 lattices + holder
StatusManuscript in prep.

Auxetic metamaterials get their behaviour from geometry, not chemistry: a re-entrant unit cell unfolds under load so the structure expands laterally as it is pulled. I worked the full loop across five designs — parameterising each cell, generating the lattice in CAD, running FEA, fabricating samples, and measuring the response on a universal testing machine.
