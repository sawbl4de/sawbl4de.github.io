Actuator

SMA-based actuator for louvre flaps

A motor-free shape-memory actuator that automates the opening and closing of louvre flaps. Built as a proof-of-concept for energy-efficient smart building envelopes (reducing cooling costs by up to 30% and heating by 10-30%), the system utilizes electrical resistive heating to contract custom-wound NiTiNOL springs and actuate the mechanical assembly, resetting automatically via a bias spring upon cooling.
